Building the Future of Offshore Marine Service's Digital Presence

A Website Development Proposal by W.A.S.P TECH for Offshores Marine Service LDA

Project Overview

Redeveloping the Offshores Marine Service LDA website based on the modern, responsive Phase 1 design, enhancing user experience and functionality.

Understanding Your Needs

Establishing a leading-edge digital platform to showcase your comprehensive services, attract new clients effectively, and solidify your position in the maritime industry.

Our Proposed Solution

W.A.S.P TECH will implement the approved Phase 1 website design, develop backend email pipelines for lead capture, ensure full functionality across devices, integrate social media links, and provide seamless support for English, Portuguese, and French languages.

Key Benefits for OMS

  • A comprehensive showcase of your services and partners.
  • An active and engaging tone to attract clients.
  • Establishing your primary digital platform for enhanced market presence.

Scope of Work & Deliverables

  • Full implementation of the Phase 1 website design (HTML, CSS, JS based on provided repo).
  • Integration with Google Apps Script for form submissions (Review & Contact/Acceptance).
  • Trilingual support (EN/PT/FR).
  • Social media integration (placeholders - links to be provided by OMS).
  • Fully responsive design for all devices.
  • Testing and deployment assistance.
  • Includes a 2-year warranty on development work.

Project Timeline

Estimated completion within one (1) month from the date of the initial 50% payment confirmation.
